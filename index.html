Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for everyone. To make sure this remains a positive space, all members, contributors, and leaders are expected to uphold the following standards:

  • Respect: Be kind, courteous, and respectful in all interactions.
  • Inclusivity: Embrace diversity and ensure equal opportunity for participation.
  • Professionalism: Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or derogatory language.
  • Collaboration: Support others by offering constructive feedback and encouragement.
  • Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and words.

Unacceptable behaviors include harassment, trolling, spamming, or any discriminatory remarks based on gender, race, religion, or identity. Violations may result in warnings, removal from discussions, or expulsion from the community. If you witness or experience misconduct, please report it to the moderation team immediately.
